# Healthcare Cybersecurity Self-Assessment Checklist

## A Quick Evaluation Guide for Healthcare Organizations

---

### How to Use This Checklist

This checklist contains **20 essential security questions** across four categories. For each question, answer:

- ✅ **Yes** — You have this covered
- ❌ **No** — This needs attention
- ❓ **Not Sure** — Needs investigation

At the end, score your organization to determine your security risk level.

## 🚨 What Your Results Mean

### If You Scored LOW RISK (18-24)
Your organization has strong security fundamentals. Continue regular security reviews and stay informed about emerging threats.

### If You Scored MODERATE RISK (12-17)
You have a solid foundation but some areas need attention. Review the sections where you answered "No" or "Not Sure" and create an improvement plan.

### If You Scored HIGH RISK (6-11)
Your organization faces real security risks. Patient data may be vulnerable. Consider a professional security assessment to identify and address gaps.

### If You Scored CRITICAL RISK (0-5)
Your organization is at serious risk of a data breach. Immediate action is required. Contact a cybersecurity professional right away.
