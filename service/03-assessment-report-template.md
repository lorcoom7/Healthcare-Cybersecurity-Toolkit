## 4. Risk Analysis

### 4.1 Risk Methodology

**Risk Score Calculation:**
```
Risk Score = Impact × Likelihood
```

**Impact Levels:**
| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Critical | Catastrophic impact on operations/patients |
| High | Significant impact requiring immediate attention |
| Medium | Moderate impact on operations |
| Low | Minimal impact, easily remediated |

**Likelihood Levels:**
| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| High | Likely to occur within 12 months |
| Medium | May occur within 12-24 months |
| Low | Unlikely to occur |

### 4.2 Risk Severity Matrix

| | Low Likelihood | Medium Likelihood | High Likelihood |
|---|----------------|-------------------|------------------|
| **Low Impact** | LOW | LOW | MEDIUM |
| **Medium Impact** | LOW | MEDIUM | HIGH |
| **High Impact** | MEDIUM | HIGH | CRITICAL |
| **Critical Impact** | HIGH | CRITICAL | CRITICAL |
