# Healthcare Cybersecurity Risk Assessment Service

---

## Service Overview

Our Healthcare Cybersecurity Risk Assessment is a comprehensive evaluation of your organization's security posture, designed specifically for healthcare providers, clinics, and telehealth companies.

This assessment identifies vulnerabilities in your systems, evaluates your HIPAA compliance status, and provides actionable recommendations to protect patient data.

### Administrative Safeguards Assessment

- Risk analysis evaluation
- Workforce security practices
- Incident response planning

### Technical Safeguards Assessment
- Access controls and authentication
- Data encryption status
- Network security configuration

- Audit logging and monitoring

### Physical Safeguards Assessment
- Facility access controls
- Workstation security

## Why It Matters

Healthcare organizations are prime targets for cyberattacks due to the sensitive nature of patient data.

- **93%** of healthcare organizations experienced a data breach in the past 5 years
- Average cost of a healthcare data breach: **$10.1 million**
- **70%** of breaches involve vulnerabilities that could be identified through assessment

This assessment helps you:
- Identify security gaps before attackers do
- Demonstrate HIPAA compliance to auditors
- Protect patient trust and reputation
- Avoid costly breach remediation
